Frequently asked questions

Are my documents uploaded anywhere? No. All PDF processing happens entirely on your device. Your documents never leave your phone. There is no account and no cloud upload.
How do I remove ads? Open the app, go to the menu, and choose "Remove Ads" — a one-time $0.99 purchase that removes ads forever. If you reinstall or switch devices, use "Restore Purchases".
How do I scan a paper document? Tap "Scan Document" on the home screen and use your camera. Pages are converted to a searchable PDF on your device.
How do I request data deletion? myAIPDF collects only anonymous crash diagnostics and an advertising identifier (for ads). No document content or personal data is collected. To make a request, email luis@lefv.io.
